## Inequalities

**Property 1.** Let

$$
\abs{a - b} \lt \abs{c}
$$

then

$$
\abs{b} - \abs{c} \lt \abs{a} \lt \abs{b} + \abs{c}
$$

**Property 2.** Suppose that for every $0 \lt \epsilon \lt 1$, the following holds:

$$
\frac{b}{1 + \epsilon} \lt \abs{a} \lt \frac{b}{1 - \epsilon}
$$

then, for every $\delta \gt 0$:

$$
\abs{a - b} \lt \delta
$$
